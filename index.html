About

Skyknock is a British Indian music producer and DJ based in London. His work is driven by a profound connection to nature, crafting immersive emotional journeys through sound.

Beginning with chill-out and ambient roots, he evolved into progressive house, shaping intricate melodies and driving rhythms. As his career progressed, he transitioned into melodic house and techno — drawing inspiration from Deadmau5, Eric Prydz, and Lane 8 — guiding listeners toward stillness, peace, and connection.

Releasing on This Never Happened, Monstercat Silk, and Purified Records, Skyknock’s music has received support from Above & Beyond, Lane 8, Ferry Corsten, Infected Mushroom, Hardwell, Tiësto, Cosmic Gate, Chicane, Steve Angello, and many others.
